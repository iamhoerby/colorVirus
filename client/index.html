Rules:

You need to pass through the magic door. As many times as you can! Use Arrows to move.

In order to do it, you and your teammate (if there is any) need to get a color that matches with color of the door.

Use Space to shoot the monsters to pick up their colors. You can bump your teammate to make a new color mix.

You have 3 lives. Each time a monster bumps into you, you lose one life and your color turns paler.

Good luck!
